About

At Peter’s, we believe that great pizza deserves great beer—and we’ve perfected the art of pairing the two. Our menu features fresh, handcrafted pizzas, each designed to shine alongside our rotating selection of craft beers. Whether you prefer a hoppy IPA to cut through cheesy goodness or a smooth lager to complement bold toppings, we’ve got the perfect pairing for every bite.
